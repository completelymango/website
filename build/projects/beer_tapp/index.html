Beer_Tapp

This is a project that several of my college friends and I worked on in 2016. Check it out!: beer.107w.io

The idea is that you, as a bar owner, can enter in all of the different beer types that you have on your taplist. Then customers can enter in a list of beers that they like (from any beer in the world) and the system will recommend a beer to them from the taplist.
